This app was developed for [ROHI Sommiers](https://rohisommiers.com/), a mattress company, to optimize their delivery processes. It is like a to-do list for the delivery and sales teams with unique features:

![Home](/all.png)

- Integrated Google Maps API for quick address searches
- One-click navigation for the delivery team
- Simplified task management for sales and delivery operations

The app significantly reduces the time needed to locate customer addresses and keep track of deliveries. With just a click on the address, the delivery team is directed to the exact location.

## Stack
- [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/) - React framework
- [Auth0](https://auth0.com) - For authentication
- [Prisma](https://www.prisma.io/) - TypeScript ORM
- [Vercel Postgres](https://vercel.com/postgres) - Easy to use Postgres database
- [shadcn/ui](https://ui.shadcn.com/) - UI components
