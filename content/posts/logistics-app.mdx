## A full-stack simple logistics app

This internally used app streamlines the process of managing mattress deliveries, enhancing both user experience and operational efficiency. The implementation of this app has led to a significant improvement in customer satisfaction and delivery tracking. The selected tech stack allows the app to be easily maintained and scaled to meet future requirements. It is already blazingly fast.

### Key Features

- Copy Delivery Address: Users can efficiently copy the delivery address with a single click. This feature minimizes manual errors and accelerates the address input process, contributing to accurate and timely deliveries.
- Call and Message Customer: The ability to directly call and message the customer from within the app simplifies communication. This feature facilitates quick updates, addresses inquiries, and ensures a smooth delivery experience.
- Mark Home Delivery as Completed: Delivery status management is streamlined by enabling users to mark mattress home deliveries as completed. This real-time update ensures accurate tracking and provides actionable insights into delivery operations.

### User Experience and Business Benefits

By combining Next.js for dynamic rendering, Tailwind CSS for responsive and visually appealing design, Prisma ORM for efficient database interactions, and Auth0 for secure user authentication, the app offers a robust, user-centric logistics management solution. The mentioned features enhance customer communication, delivery tracking, and operational efficiency, ultimately leading to improved customer satisfaction and optimized delivery processes.

It is a really simple app, but as a product it is really useful. It is heavily used by [ROHI Sommiers](https://rohisommiers.com) and it is a key part of the delivery process.
