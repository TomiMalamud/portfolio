This app was developed for [ROHI Sommiers](https://rohisommiers.com/), a mattress company, to optimize their delivery processes. It is like a to-do list for the delivery and sales teams with unique features:

![Home](/all.png)

- Integrated with the company's ERP system to fetch sales and delivery data.
- One-click navigation for the delivery team
- Simplified task management for sales and delivery operations

It optimizes the delivery process by providing the delivery team with a list of tasks, including the delivery address, contact information, and a one-click navigation button to open Google Maps with the delivery address.
It is integrated with the company's ERP system, which includes sales, products and delivery data.

## Stack
- [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/) - React framework
- [Supabase](https://supabase.com/) - Postgres database and Auth
- [shadcn/ui](https://ui.shadcn.com/) - UI components
